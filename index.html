03 | May's Empanadas

Role: UX/UI Researcher and Promotional Manager
Year: July 2020-December 2020
Skills: WebFlow, Figma, Adobe Suite, Google Suite, Canva, Wireframing

Overview

May’s Empanadas is a one-woman owned local food-catering service in my neighborhood. Through casual phone calls and texts, the business owner receives orders and supplies homemade batches of empanadas across Queens, New York. Along with other Filipino delicacies, the hearty empanadas spread through word of mouth across the borough, and the owner now receives an abundant flow of order requests from loyal Filipino customers. As a first-generation Filipino college student, I was looking forward to modernizing this business to match the pace of its well-deserved, growing success.

Goals

Design an interface that allows customers to quickly and effortlessly access products and place orders.
Design an interface that was equally as efficient for the founder to review orders and make informed business decisions based on customer data.
Experience Goal: Develop a highly intuitive order application. Design Goal: Design a compelling visual brand identity that communicates to its target audience. Personal Goal: Shed light to a local business that is close to heart.
